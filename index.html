Hi, I'm Ben!

I'm a computer programmer (beep boop)! I'm sure you know the type.

I graduated from Virginia Tech in May of 2022 with a BS in Computer Science. You can check out some details about my coursework and class projects on the Education page.

During that time, I was an intern for the OpenNMS Group! You can read a bit about my work for them on my resume. I was also an instructor for iD Tech Camps for two summers, which was a lot of fun.

Programming is my passion. I like learning new things, and then using those new things to make other new things. Fred Brooks puts it best.

Here are a few of those things I've made:

  • Plateau, a 2-dimensional life simulation game. This is the one I'm most proud of, even if it still isn't finished (and maybe never will be). I set out to make a video game on my own, teaching myself the basics of game development (using C# and the MonoGame framework), pixel art and music production as the need arose. I also quickly learned why trying to work on even a small game by yourself is a terrible, terrible idea. Much more details on the Plateau page.
  • SheriffScorer, an Android scoresheet app for the Sheriff of Nottingham board game. I taught myself some Android development for this one.
    10K+ downloads on Google Play, 4.5 ★ average review.
  • This website! I didn't have the chance to learn any web development while working on my undergraduate degree, but the plethora of online resources made it easy to learn the basics!
  • Check out some other projects on the Projects page!

Wanna chat? The best way to contact me is through email. Thanks for stopping by!
